When you're living in London, Houston, Toronto, or Dubai — your property, your assets, and your family are still in Nigeria. You deserve to know the truth about what's happening there, verified by professionals bound by law to tell you exactly that.

Project Supervision is LAPEQ's flagship diaspora service: a structured, recurring property and asset oversight program delivered by dedicated ground agents, documented with photo and video evidence, and reported to you on a monthly cadence with absolute confidentiality.

"We don't just tell you everything is fine.
We show you — with evidence."
Every Project Supervision report is verified, signed off by a senior LAPEQ Diaspora Manager, and delivered as a formatted document you can access on any device. Issues are flagged the moment they're discovered, not buried in a monthly summary.

Enrol in Project Supervision
01
Property Oversight
Physical inspection of your residential or commercial property, including structure, security, utilities, and grounds — with timestamped photo documentation.

02
Caretaker Accountability
Independent verification of your caretaker, groundsmen, or property managers. We report on their presence, conduct, and the condition they keep your property in.

03
Rental Income Verification
For leased properties, we verify tenant occupancy, rent payment status, property condition, and any lease violations — documented and reported to you monthly.

04
Emergency Response
When something goes wrong — flood, security incident, structural issue — our ground team responds within 24 hours, documents the scene, and coordinates resolution.

05
Family Welfare Checks
Discreet, caring welfare visits to elderly parents or family members. We confirm wellbeing, coordinate care needs, and report with warmth and professionalism.

The Process
How Project Supervision Works
From your first consultation to your first report — here's exactly what happens when you enrol in Project Supervision.

I
Intake Call
You speak with a Diaspora Manager who maps your property, assets, and any concerns. Everything is covered by NDA before you say a word.

II
Agent Assignment
A dedicated ground agent is assigned to your location. They are background-checked, trained, and bound by a personal NDA to your account only.

III
First Inspection
Your agent conducts a full initial inspection within 72 hours, establishing a baseline condition record for all assets on your portfolio.

IV
Monthly Reports
Every month, a formatted Project Supervision Report is delivered to you — with photos, condition ratings, incident notes, and a summary from your Diaspora Manager.

V
Real-Time Alerts
Any issue flagged between reports is communicated to you immediately, with a WhatsApp + email summary and a recommended action plan from our team.
